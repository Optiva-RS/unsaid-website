Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Unsaid ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. Unsaid is designed to be a private and secure AI-powered journaling platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and protect your information when you use the Unsaid mobile application and related services (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

We believe journaling is deeply personal.

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not sell your journal entries.
  • We do not use your content for advertising.
  • Your entries are used to power and personalize your experience within Unsaid.

Your content belongs to you.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

  • Name, if provided
  • Email address
  • Login credentials
  • User preferences

B. Journal & Reflection Content

  • Journal entries
  • Prompts and AI-generated responses
  • Saved reflections
  • Private folders and locked entries

Your journal content may include sensitive personal information depending on what you choose to share.

C. Technical & Usage Data

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • IP address
  • General location, such as city or state level
  • App usage analytics

D. Payment Information

If you subscribe to paid features, payments are processed by Apple or other third-party app stores. We do not store full credit card information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service
  • Generate AI-powered responses
  • Personalize your AI experience
  • Improve the relevance of future responses for you
  • Maintain security
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Personalization & Processing

Unsaid uses artificial intelligence to generate responses to your journal entries. Your journal entries may be processed to:

  • Generate immediate AI responses
  • Improve personalization of your future AI interactions
  • Enhance your individual AI experience over time

Your entries help your AI better understand your patterns, tone, and preferences.

We do not sell your journal entries. We do not use your private content for advertising. We do not publicly display your entries.

We may use aggregated, anonymized data that cannot reasonably identify you to improve overall system performance and reliability. AI processing occurs using secure infrastructure providers.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. Security measures may include encrypted data transmission, secure cloud infrastructure, access controls, and authentication safeguards.

However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information while your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with cloud hosting providers, analytics providers, payment processors, and legal authorities if required by law. All service providers are required to maintain appropriate security safeguards.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access your personal data, correct inaccurate information, request deletion, request a copy of your data, or withdraw consent where applicable.

You may exercise these rights by contacting us using the information below.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Unsaid does not sell personal information as defined under CCPA.

10. Children's Privacy

Unsaid is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If discovered, such information will be deleted.

11. International Users

If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

12. Not a HIPAA-Covered Entity

Unsaid is not a healthcare provider and is not a covered entity under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). The Service is not intended for storing protected health information under HIPAA regulations.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will revise the "Last Updated" date when changes are made. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
