Support

Need help with Unsaid, your subscription, or your account? Start here.

Contact support

Email: hello@showcasesolutionsapps.com

When you reach out, include the email address used for your Unsaid account, your device type, and a short description of what happened.

Subscription help

Unsaid subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel, renew, or review billing, open your Apple ID subscription settings on your iPhone.

  • Open Settings on your iPhone.
  • Tap your name.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Choose Unsaid.

Restore purchases

If premium access does not appear after subscribing, open Unsaid and use the restore purchases option on the subscription screen.

Safety resources

Unsaid is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or may harm yourself or someone else, call emergency services. In the United States, call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
